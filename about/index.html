I’m a PhD candidate in Political Science. I wrote my dissertation on the concept of “domestic colonialism” in the United States, asking whether it could (or should) be reconstructed as an element of anticolonial critical theory today.

My research and reading interests are in:

  • Empire, race, and colonialism in political theory
  • Philosophy and history of social science
  • Dialectical philosophy and its relation to philosophy of history
  • Twentieth-century engagements with radicalism in political theory
